The Pillars of Singularity

Our "Hub-and-Spoke" structure fosters specialized expertise within a unified, collaborative ecosystem. Each division is a critical component of our mission to learn by building.

The Finance Division ("The Quants")

Role: The club's core identity. They apply AI to real-world financial analysis and investment strategy.

  • Running a simulated investment fund.
  • Using AI tools to analyze companies and markets.
  • Designing innovative trading strategies.

The AI Division ("The Engine Room")

Role: The technical heart of the club, focused on building the AI skills needed for projects.

  • Mastering no-code AI tools and prompt engineering.
  • Learning Python and machine learning for finance.
  • Developing bespoke AI solutions for projects.

The Consulting Division ("The Ambassadors")

Role: The club's bridge to the real world, applying members' skills to solve actual problems.

  • Free "AI Strategy" consulting for campus clubs/startups.
  • Representing Singularity in business case competitions.
  • Building a portfolio of client testimonials.

The Operations Division ("The Foundation")

Role: The essential backbone that ensures the club is run professionally and continues to grow.

  • Handling all marketing, branding, and recruitment.
  • Managing events, budgets, and internal affairs.
  • Fostering a strong alumni network.
